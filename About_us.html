About us

In Essence of art, we display art to the wider public. Here, we believe that art is not only to be enjoyed by its aesthetic beauty but to see the experiences, perspectives, emotions, as well as to travel time. In this museum, we also explore how art has evolved throughout the years and how it affects society as a whole.
